Welcome to my homepage! I am a 1st year Graduate student in Computer Science at Virginia Tech. My advisor is Prof. Ramamoorthi and I work in the Visual Computing Group. I'm currently an intern at Nvidia. I'm graduating in Spring 2015.

My current work is based on a wide-cross section of areas in computer science including computer vision, crowdsourcing,machine learning, information storage and retrieval and internet software. Specifically I use techniques of content-based image retrieval and crowd intelligence to make sense of visual data. One interesting spin-off of this research is to be able to analyze arbitrarily complex images(images about which nothing is known) and be able to predict information about the image.I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering and a Master's in Mathematics fom BITS, Pilani, India.
