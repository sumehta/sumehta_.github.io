Welcome to my homepage!

I am a 1st year Graduate student in Computer Science at Virginia Tech. My current work is at the intersection of computer vision, crowdsourcing and machine learning. In my research assistantship with Prof. Kurt Luther I am combining techniques in content-based image retrieval and crowdsourced human intelligence to make sense of visual data. One interesting outcome of this research is to be able to analyze and describe arbitrarily complex images (given little or no context) and be able to predict information about the image.

Work Experience

I have worked as a Research & Development engineer at Dreamworks Animation SKG where I worked on a task graph based execution framework for creating and executing complex workflows. The framework lets the user create complex task graphs with each node representing a unit of work and edges representing data dependencies between nodes. It also lets the user deploy the graph to the farm(computing cluster) for execution. I worked with technologies like Python, PyQt, and Pytest-qt.
